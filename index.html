Bridging Loans

With a bridging loan, the decision to buy/sell or sell/buy can be lessened. In most cases, people will sell their existing home first, and then use the proceeds to purchase a new one. Bridging loans can help in these situations. Bridging loans are short-term loans used to finance the purchase of a new property while your current property is being sold. 

We at Clark Finance Group are here to help you navigate the bridging finance process, whether you are just getting started or have already signed the dotted line on your new home. Providing you with the right finance solution is their goal, and they want to make sure your journey is successful.
